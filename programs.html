Health Coaching

Nutrition: Emphasize a balanced diet rich in iron, calcium, and vitamins. Encourage whole foods and hydration to support overall well-being.

Exercise: Suggest regular physical activity to alleviate cramps and improve mood. Yoga or gentle exercises may be particularly beneficial.

Management: Teach stress reduction techniques like deep breathing, meditation, or mindfulness to help manage stress, which can impact menstrual health.

Hygiene: Emphasize the importance of sufficient and quality sleep, as it contributes to hormonal balance and overall health.

Hygiene Practices: Educate on proper menstrual hygiene, including the use of sanitary products and maintaining cleanliness to prevent infections.

Tracking Menstrual Cycle:Tracking Menstrual Cycle: Encourage the use of menstrual tracking apps to monitor cycles, symptoms, and identify patterns. This can be helpful for understanding irregularities.

Consultation: Advise seeking medical advice for persistent issues or significant changes in menstrual patterns. Regular check-ups are essential for women's health.

Hormonal Balance:Hormonal Balance: Discuss lifestyle factors that may impact hormonal balance and explore ways to support hormonal health naturally.)
